## Post-Pulse Operating Window

Several operating years after the Pulse, compromised Zones remain active in
ordinary places: clinics, towers, transit corridors, service tunnels, research
campuses, churches, hospitals, suburbs, and commercial districts that have
begun to remember too much.

VeilBorn operators are not exceptional by permission. They are functional by
necessity. Some are contractors. Some are survivors. Some are specialists. Some
are altered enough to remain useful where consensus behavior fails.

Entry is never conquest.

Entry is recovery under notice.

## Primary Procedure

Enter.

Listen before moving.

Recover only what can be carried without becoming too clear.

Avoid teaching the Zone your shape.

Leave before the local environment completes classification.

## Survival Pressures

Field reports consistently identify three pressure categories:

| Pressure | Operational Question | Failure Pattern |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Body integrity | Can the environment kill you? | Bleeding, impact, exposure, collapse, pursuit. |
| Cognitive coherence | Can you still trust your own position? | False routes, unreliable prompts, mirrored rooms, teammate mismatch. |
| Observed relevance | Has the Zone learned enough to resolve you? | Escalation, pursuit, exit instability, personal debt. |

These pressures interact. A quiet injury may remain local. A loud injury may
become procedural.

## Reflective Surface Advisory

Mirrors, dark windows, standing water, polished metal, surveillance glass, and
black-screen devices are not passive surfaces inside an active Zone.

They may reveal what the room refuses to state directly.

They may also notice that they have been consulted.

Do not stare for confirmation unless confirmation is worth the invoice.

## Interface Trust Advisory

At elevated distortion, trusted systems may present correct formatting with
incorrect intent.

Do not follow a teammate marker without confirming the teammate.

Do not accept an exit prompt unless the exit still agrees with the floor.

Do not assume a familiar voice is attached to a familiar body.

If two instruments disagree, reduce movement, reduce light, reduce noise, and
ask what changed after you looked.

## Recovery Conduct

Resources can be useful.

Relics can be useful.

Evidence can be useful.

Myth-tech fragments can be useful.

Usefulness is not safety. Every recovered object carries history, pressure, and
the possibility of recognition. If the Zone understands why you want something,
it may begin pricing the route around that desire.

## Extraction Conduct

Extraction is conditional forgiveness.

Tunnels, lifts, service routes, hatches, private keys, and reflective exits can
all function under favorable conditions. None are owed to the operator.

Gunfire near an exit teaches the exit that it matters.

Explosions near an exit may convince the exit to stop being a place.

Sabotage is possible. So is being left inside the consequence.

## Convergence Warning

A Zone does not simply become harder over time.

It becomes more certain.

Doors reduce disagreement. Distances revise themselves. Reflections stop
copying. The unstable layer gains procedural authority. Remaining personnel may
be reassigned from visitor to resident without notification.

There is no final victory condition inside a waking Zone.

There is only recovery, cost, and departure.

## Operator Reminder

Fear is information, not an instruction.

Run if necessary.

Hide if plausible.

Fight only if the debt is acceptable.

Take only what you can carry through the consequence.

Leave before the world finishes waking up.
