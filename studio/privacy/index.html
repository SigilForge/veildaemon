Privacy notice

How the Studio site on veildaemon.app handles analytics and inquiry contact. Effective date: July 12, 2026. This notice may be updated as the studio’s operations evolve.

Analytics

Studio pages use Vercel Web Analytics. Aggregated traffic signals help understand which pages are read. Vercel’s analytics design does not rely on third-party advertising cookies. No advertising network trackers are intentionally embedded on Studio pages.

Cookies

We do not intentionally set marketing or advertising cookies on Studio pages. Platform or analytics infrastructure may use short-lived technical identifiers as described by the host provider. VeilDaemon product surfaces use separate local storage and voluntary report systems described in the VeilDaemon Privacy Policy.

Inquiry email

Messages sent via mailto: links go to the founder contact address. Content is used to respond to funding, publishing, press, technology, venue, and partnership inquiries. Do not send sensitive credentials through email.

Data sale

Cradlepoint Studio does not sell personal inquiry data or analytics data.
