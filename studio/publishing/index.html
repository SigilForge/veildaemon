Publishing opportunity

A partner accelerates an existing line

Already in production

Book One on the Published Shelf (PDF + wallpaper pack), published Operator and Handler cores, free and paid Needlepoints, digital asset previews, a governed rules corpus, live Operator/Handler session tools with VeilLink logins, public archive, and existing product art.

Partner contribution

Professional editing, layout, commissioned art, print expertise, fulfillment planning, distribution, campaign support, or audience reach against a defined package.

Negotiable scope

Budget, format, print quantity, territory, rights, exclusivity, marketing obligations, approval, reporting, and reversion remain route-specific negotiation points.

Expansion result

A professionally finished and distributed tabletop line with recurring case releases—not funding used to invent the first viable product.

Decision paths

Choose the route around an existing catalog

Route A

Direct independent

Digital PDFs and print-on-demand preserve control, rights, customer learning, and incremental release speed. Studio carries edit, layout, art direction, and discoverability.

Route B

Crowdfunded print

A campaign can fund editing, layout, art, proofs, manufacturing, fulfillment preparation, and a defined launch cohort. Campaign planning must model platform fees, production, shipping, fulfillment, taxes, and contingency separately from gross pledges.

Route C

Publishing partner

Advance or contribution may trade margin and some control for production, retail reach, marketing, or operational load reduction. Rights floors apply.

Decision gates

When to advance each route

Professional digital

Editorial scope, contractor quotes, launch plan, free→paid path instrumented.

Crowdfunding

Print-ready scope, supplier and fulfillment quotes, prelaunch threshold, shipping policy, contingency.

Partnership

Rights floor, economics, operational contribution, distribution evidence, reversion protection.

Scale

Repeat sales, contribution margin, acquisition evidence, sustained cadence.
