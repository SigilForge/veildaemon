Process

Five steps. No black box.

  1. 01
    Review

    You explain what the business does, what is failing online today, and what a good outcome looks like—calls, quotes, bookings, visits.

  2. 02
    Scope

    You get a recommended package, deliverables, timeline, and any third-party costs before work starts—so the headache is priced, not discovered mid-build.

  3. 03
    Build

    Structure, design, and content are assembled so a first-time visitor can understand the offer and take the next step.

  4. 04
    Revise

    You review a real draft and return consolidated feedback within the included rounds—changes that improve clarity, not endless redesign loops.

  5. 05
    Launch

    The site goes live on your domain and is checked on desktop and mobile so “done” means customers can use it.

Projects require clear scope approval and an initial deposit before work begins.

Frequently asked questions

Straight answers.

Do I need to already own a domain?

No. You can purchase one during the project. Domain and DNS setup assistance is available as an optional add-on. Registration fees are paid by the client to the registrar.

Can you improve my existing website?

Often, yes—if the current stack can be cleaned up efficiently. Sometimes a rebuild is faster and more maintainable than patching a confusing or broken site. Scope is recommended after review.

Do you provide hosting?

SigilForge Studios can recommend, configure, or work with hosting, but ongoing hosting and platform charges are normally paid by the client. Hosting is not bundled as an unlimited free service.

Will I be able to update the site?

Yes. Sites are built so common changes are realistic for the owner. Optional Care Plans cover updates for you—month-to-month—so you never have to learn DNS because a form stopped emailing. Bigger work is quoted, not ambushed.

Do I have to buy a care plan?

No. Launch is complete without one. Care is optional insurance after the site is live: backups, monitoring, small updates, and help when something breaks. Cancel anytime. No hostage contracts.

Do you build ecommerce sites?

When catalog and checkout needs are clear, yes—as a scoped add-on. It is not part of Launch or Business packages by default.

Do you guarantee Google rankings?

No. The site is built so search engines can understand the business from day one. Rankings still depend on competition, location, content, and time. Guarantees are not offered.

Can you write the website copy?

Yes. Packages include refining your words so visitors understand the offer. Drafting from rough notes is available when writing is the bottleneck.

Can you use my existing logo and photos?

Yes, when you have the rights to use them. Logo cleanup is available if the mark needs to look sharp online.

How long does a site take?

A focused Launch page can often move in a few weeks. Business and Custom take longer. Speed depends on scope, content readiness, and how quickly feedback comes back.

Why is the pricing lower than a traditional agency?

Because SigilForge Studios is building its external client portfolio and keeps delivery direct. You work with the person building the site. Introductory rates on limited projects—not a race to the bottom, and not a disposable process. See Why the pricing is lower.

I’d rather build ten websites that genuinely help local businesses than a hundred forgettable templates. Every project is scoped individually and built with long-term maintainability in mind.
