Frequently asked questions

Straight answers.

Do I need to already own a domain?

No. You can purchase one during the project. Domain and DNS setup assistance is available as an optional add-on. Registration fees are paid by the client to the registrar.

Can you improve my existing website?

Often, yes—if the current stack can be cleaned up efficiently. Sometimes a rebuild is faster and more maintainable than patching a confusing or broken site. Scope is recommended after review.

Do you provide hosting?

Cradlepoint Studio can recommend, configure, or work with hosting, but ongoing hosting and platform charges are normally paid by the client. Hosting is not bundled as an unlimited free service.

Will I be able to update the site?

Yes, within a realistic scope. Sites are structured so owners can update common content without depending on the Studio for every small change. More complex edits can be handled under maintenance or a separate estimate.

Do you build ecommerce sites?

Ecommerce can be scoped when the catalog and checkout needs are clear. It is not part of the base Launch or Small Business packages and is quoted separately.

Do you guarantee Google rankings?

No. The service includes sound technical foundations and content structure that search engines can understand. Rankings depend on competition, location, content quality, and factors outside any single site build. Guaranteed rankings are not offered.

Can you write the website copy?

Yes. Packages include copy refinement. Writing from rough notes or a fuller content pass is available as an add-on when you need more drafting support.

Can you use my existing logo and photos?

Yes, when you have the rights to use them. You confirm ownership or license of supplied images, logos, and text. Logo cleanup is available if the file needs polishing.

How long does a site take?

A focused Launch Page can often move in a few weeks. Multi-page and custom projects take longer. Timeline depends on scope, content readiness, and how quickly feedback returns.

Why is the pricing lower than a traditional agency?

Introductory portfolio rates are available while Cradlepoint Studio builds its external client portfolio. The lower price reflects capacity and portfolio stage, not a disposable process. Work is still scoped, revised, and launched with care.
