Cradlepoint Studio is an independent creative technology studio built around original transmedia horror-fantasy IP.

The studio develops tabletop publishing (Operator and Handler products, free and paid Needlepoint case modules), live Operator and Handler session software (deliberate Cell sync during play; multi-device via VeilLink logins and table live-link), Book One and digital shelf assets, fiction, marketing and archive media, and a longer-horizon Mobile AR / Ritual Sites direction.

Cradlepoint Studio’s commercial thesis is that tabletop, fiction, software identity, and physical-world participation are usually sold as disconnected products. Cradlepoint Studio connects them through one persistent emotional-physics system so each surface can reinforce the others.

J. Donavon Love is the systems designer, creative director, and canon authority. AI assists drafting and retrieval inside a governed pipeline; human release approval retains authority.

Documented internal production evidence (public figures rounded, with periods disclosed separately) includes 350K+ core rules words, 25 current final release PDFs, 130+ public wiki articles, live VeilDaemon infrastructure with an automated browser test suite, nearly 1K marketing images, and $0 paid acquisition through the July 10, 2026 snapshot.
