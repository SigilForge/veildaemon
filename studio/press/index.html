Medium — download .txt 
Cradlepoint Studio is an independent creative technology studio building connected fiction, tabletop systems, companion software, and location-based interactive experiences.

Its work centers on one emotional-physics framework expressed as playable procedure (Cradlepoint TTRPG and Needlepoints), software identity and Handler tools (VeilDaemon), fiction, and a longer-horizon Mobile AR direction.

The studio is solo-founded by J. Donavon Love, with a governed production pipeline and public evidence of shipping products, live software, and a substantial audited rules corpus—without paid acquisition.

Brand architecture

Real-world vs in-universe marks

Publishers and partners should not have to guess who they are negotiating with. Keep these identities separate in captions and contracts.

Cradlepoint Studio

Real-world studio and business identity. Use the Cradlepoint Studio logo files above for external partnerships; keep VeilCorp marks diegetic.

VeilDaemon

Public product / platform. Use software hero art and product UI imagery to represent the live system.

VeilCorp

Fictional in-universe organization. Marks below are diegetic brand language—not a real-world endorser or contracting party.
