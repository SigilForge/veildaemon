About

Translation before mythology.

Cradlepoint began as a systems-design problem: make invisible internal experience legible enough to reason about together.

Why the studio exists

Neurodivergent support-system thinking led to emotional physics: a shared framework for pressure, memory, identity, and connection. Fiction became its narrative interface. The TTRPG made it shared procedure. VeilDaemon became its software interface. Mobile AR extends it into physical space.

How it operates

J. Donavon Love is the systems designer, creative director, and canon authority. AI assists drafting and retrieval inside a governed production pipeline; separate review passes and human release approval retain authority.

“The translation came first. The mythology came later.”
