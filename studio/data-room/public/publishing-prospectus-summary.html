Thesis

Professional publishing converts an existing rules system, content library, software companion, and release pipeline into a clearer flagship product—not a concept invented from zero.

Beachhead lifecycle

Free Needlepoint → Operator or Handler Pack → Core / campaign material → VeilDaemon account → continuing experiences.

Routes

Direct independent — digital and POD; control and learning speed

Crowdfunded print — campaign funds production and identifies a launch cohort

Publishing partnership — advance or contribution vs margin and rights tradeoffs

Near-term revenue engine

Tabletop core products + recurring Needlepoints + crowdfunding/print.

Premium VeilDaemon content and events follow. Mobile AR is later upside.

Decision gates

Professional digital scope and quotes · crowdfunding prelaunch threshold · partnership rights floor · scale only with margin and cadence evidence.
