What it is

A tech-enabled mythpunk studio using one governed story-and-rules framework to produce fiction, tabletop products, live software, and eventually location-based interactive experiences.

What already exists

  • Shipping free and paid tabletop products
  • Live VeilDaemon companion software
  • Internally audited rules corpus and multi-book manuscripts
  • Public wiki and marketing library
  • Governed production pipeline with human release authority
  • $0 paid acquisition to date

Commercial sequence

Near-term: tabletop core + Needlepoints + crowdfunding/print

Mid-term: premium VeilDaemon content + events

Upside: Mobile AR after audience and IP pipeline

Funding (public)

  • $67,000 / 12 months studio growth envelope (capacity, art, specialists, infrastructure, print, media, conventions, fiction)
  • Publishing production and Mobile AR MVP are separate scoped budgets
  • Optional ≤$20K bounded proof-of-concept contribution is not the studio plan
