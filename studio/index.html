04 // MOBILE AR

It does not add fantasy to the city. It removes the filter.

VeilDaemon Mobile AR connects physical locations, modular encounters, Operator identity, live events, and the existing platform.

  1. 01Discover

    Find a location, event, signal, or physical glyph.

  2. 02Activate

    Unlock a location-aware encounter packet.

  3. 03Reveal

    Enter an AR environment already present in the fiction.

  4. 04Resolve

    Complete the encounter and record its consequence.

  5. 05Persist

    Carry identity, artifact, cosmetic, clue, or world-state forward.

COMMERCIAL HYPOTHESIS

Recurring software revenue becomes possible—but only after the product earns the right to scale.

Cosmetics, visual-effect packs, premium encounters, event passes, venue activations, and commissioned experiences are testable revenue lanes. Paid acquisition begins only when measured retention and contribution lifetime value support it.
