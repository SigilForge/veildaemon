Technology + Mobile AR

The interface removes the filter.

VeilDaemon connects Operator identity, narrative state, Handler tools, and a future location-based encounter layer.

Revenue sequence

Near-term commercial engine is tabletop + Needlepoints + crowdfunding/print. Premium VeilDaemon content and events come next. Cosmetics, encounter packs, event passes, and venue activations remain hypotheses until piloted—AR is upside on an existing audience, not the first engine.

What exists now

VeilDaemon is functional browser-based infrastructure for intake, local records, play support, reporting, and Handler operation. Current public evidence includes more than 50 JavaScript modules and more than 100 passing browser tests.

What comes next

Mobile AR is being scoped as a commercially testable product: location activation, reusable encounter packets, Operator continuity, analytics, accessibility, and a limited monetization experiment.

Commercial hypothesis

Cosmetics, effect packs, premium encounters, event passes, venue activations, and commissioned experiences remain hypotheses. Paid acquisition begins only when retention and contribution lifetime value support it.

Validation

Activation, completion, reliability, Day 1/7/30 retention, repeat sessions, payer behavior, infrastructure cost, accessibility, CPI, LTV-to-CAC, and payback determine whether scaling is supportable.
