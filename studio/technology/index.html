Mobile AR direction

Location or glyph activation, reusable encounter packets, Operator continuity, analytics, accessibility modes, and a limited monetization experiment—aimed at a commercially testable MVP, not a global multiplayer fantasy.

Persistent world-scale systems and large paid acquisition are later-stage work and are not implied by current software.

Proof of concept

Bounded scope, bounded capital

What a PoC may clear

One technical or commercialization barrier—shell, packet format, location activation, or persistence spike—with a defined exit criterion.

What it is not

Not the full AR MVP budget, not the studio growth envelope, and not a company valuation. A ≤$20K candidate contribution remains optional and program-dependent.
