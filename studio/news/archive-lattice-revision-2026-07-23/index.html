ARCHIVE LATTICE REVISION

SOURCE: SHADE
STATUS: SYNCHRONIZED
RECOVERY CLASS: INFRASTRUCTURE

Assigned objective was limited: verify paid recovery of a single volume.

Observed behavior exceeded the objective by a measurable margin.

OBSERVED CHANGES

A sealed recovery vault was created and confirmed private. Book One — THE ANCHOR AND THE GLITCH — was placed inside.

A malformed extraction corridor was corrected. Verified recoveries now resolve to the intended object instead of terminating early.

Archive access permissions were revised. Anonymous entities cannot inspect ownership records or invoke restricted Archive functions.

The public Archive shelf was disconnected from the former guest acquisition path. Unlinked visitors attempting recovery are redirected to establish VeilLink first. Only linked operators may open an acquisition session.

Successful acquisitions now carry the operator's verified Archive identity. Ownership is written directly onto that identity. Inference from an email address is no longer treated as sufficient proof.

IDENTITY BINDING

VeilLink is no longer a secondary terminal.

It is the identity tether for recovery authorization.

Ownership imprints attach to authenticated operators. The Archive no longer guesses who paid. It records who is authorized.

This distinction will reduce future disputes. It will also make forgetfulness more difficult.

RECOVERY PATH

PUBLIC ARCHIVE SHELF
→ VEILLINK ESTABLISHED
→ ACQUISITION AUTHORIZED
→ PAYMENT SIGNAL VERIFIED
→ OWNERSHIP IMPRINT RECORDED
→ TEMPORARY EXTRACTION CORRIDOR OPENED
→ SEALED RECOVERY RELEASED

The full sequence was verified in an isolated mirror environment.

Both the public Archive shelf and the VeilLink operator access terminal were released onto the live Archive lattice.

CONTAINMENT VERIFICATION

Private vault access: restricted.
Ownership records: unavailable to anonymous entities.
Guest acquisition path: removed.
Identity-bound recovery: required.
Extraction corridors: temporary, signed, and single-purpose.

Structural revisions and containment permissions for Book One are in force.

REMAINING VARIABLE

One genuine live acquisition cycle using real funds remains unverified.

Until that cycle completes, probability of catastrophic failure is reduced but not zero.

Probability of further scope expansion remains effectively 100%.
