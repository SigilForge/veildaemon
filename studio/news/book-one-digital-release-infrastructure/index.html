Book One Digital Release Infrastructure Complete

July 23, 2026 · Publishing · Platform

← Studio News

Today marks the completion of the first full version of SigilForge Studios's digital publishing infrastructure.

Although the original objective was simply to deliver The Cradlepoint Archive: Book One after purchase, the work expanded into a complete ownership and delivery system that will support future books, game materials, dossiers, audio releases, and other Archive recoveries.

Today's work included:

  • Private digital delivery using secure storage
  • Identity-bound ownership through VeilLink
  • Removal of guest checkout
  • Secure entitlement tracking
  • Hardened access permissions
  • Verified recovery pipeline from purchase through download
  • Deployment of the complete infrastructure to the live platform

Every future digital release will build upon this same framework.

Remaining Verification

The only remaining launch task is one genuine production purchase to validate the complete live acquisition cycle.

Everything else has now been verified locally and deployed to production.

Book One is no longer simply a PDF.

It is the first recovery inside the Archive.
